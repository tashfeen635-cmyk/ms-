Our Academic Programs

Modern School offers a rigorous and well-rounded curriculum across three stages of learning, each designed to meet the developmental and intellectual needs of every student.

Primary School Program

Our primary school program focuses on foundational learning through a play-based and activity-driven curriculum. We believe that young learners absorb knowledge best when they are engaged, curious, and having fun. Our classrooms are vibrant spaces where children explore, experiment, and grow at their own pace.

The curriculum integrates hands-on activities, storytelling, creative arts, and collaborative projects to develop strong literacy, numeracy, and social skills. Every child receives individual attention from our trained educators who are passionate about early childhood development.

Core Subjects

  • English Language & Literature
  • Hindi Language
  • Mathematics
  • Environmental Science (EVS)
  • General Knowledge
  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Art & Craft
  • Physical Education & Yoga
  • Music & Dance
  • Value Education & Life Skills

Secondary School Program

The secondary school curriculum at Modern School provides a comprehensive and academically rigorous learning experience. Students are introduced to specialized subjects in science, mathematics, social sciences, and languages, building a strong intellectual foundation for higher studies.

Our pedagogical approach emphasizes critical thinking, analytical reasoning, and problem-solving. Regular assessments, lab sessions, research projects, and inter-school competitions ensure that students develop both theoretical knowledge and practical competencies.

Core Subjects

  • English Language & Literature
  • Hindi / Sanskrit (Second Language)
  • Mathematics
  • Science (Physics, Chemistry, Biology)
  • Social Science (History, Geography, Civics, Economics)
  • Computer Science & Information Technology
  • Third Language (French / German)
  • Art Education
  • Physical Education & Sports
  • Work Experience & Community Service

Senior Secondary Program

The senior secondary program prepares students for university education and professional careers through specialized academic streams. Students choose from Science, Commerce, or Humanities based on their aptitude and career aspirations. Each stream is taught by subject-matter experts and supported by modern laboratories, libraries, and digital resources.

We also provide comprehensive career counselling, competitive exam preparation, and mentorship programs to help students make informed decisions about their futures.

Science Stream

  • Physics
  • Chemistry
  • Mathematics / Biology
  • Computer Science / Informatics Practices
  • English Core

Commerce Stream

  • Accountancy
  • Business Studies
  • Economics
  • Mathematics / Informatics Practices
  • English Core

Humanities Stream

  • History
  • Political Science
  • Geography / Sociology
  • Psychology / Economics
  • English Core

Curriculum Highlights

Beyond textbooks and exams, our curriculum is enriched with programs that develop well-rounded individuals ready for real-world challenges.

STEM Education

Our integrated STEM program combines science, technology, engineering, and mathematics through project-based learning. Students gain hands-on experience with modern tools and technologies that prepare them for innovation-driven careers.

  • Robotics and coding labs from Grade 3 onwards
  • Annual science exhibitions and hackathons
  • Collaboration with IITs and tech companies for mentorship
  • Dedicated AI and machine learning elective for senior students

Language Arts

Our language arts program cultivates strong communication skills, creative expression, and cultural literacy. Students develop fluency in multiple languages and build confidence in public speaking, creative writing, and critical analysis.

  • Trilingual proficiency: English, Hindi, and a foreign language
  • Debate club, Model United Nations, and public speaking workshops
  • Annual literary festival with renowned authors
  • Student-run school magazine and creative writing journal

Life Skills

We believe education extends beyond academic knowledge. Our life skills program equips students with essential competencies such as leadership, emotional intelligence, financial literacy, and community responsibility.

  • Leadership and team-building workshops every semester
  • Community service projects and social outreach initiatives
  • Financial literacy and entrepreneurship modules
  • Mindfulness, mental health awareness, and wellness sessions
