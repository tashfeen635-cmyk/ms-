Requirements & Eligibility

Please ensure you meet the following criteria before applying.

Documents Required

  • Birth certificate
  • Previous school report card
  • Transfer certificate
  • 4 passport-size photographs
  • Aadhar card copy (student & parent)
  • Address proof

Age Criteria

  • Nursery: 3+ years as of March 31
  • KG: 4+ years as of March 31
  • Class 1: 5+ years as of March 31
  • Class 2-8: Age appropriate
  • Class 9-10: Based on previous marks
  • Class 11-12: Based on board results

Selection Criteria

  • Written aptitude test
  • Interaction with the student
  • Parent-teacher meeting
  • Previous academic record
  • Extra-curricular achievements
  • Sibling preference applicable

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about the admission process.

The admission process for the new academic year typically begins in November and continues until March. Early applications are encouraged as seats are limited and allocated on a first-come, first-served basis after meeting eligibility criteria.
